Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do you offer International Shipping?

Yes, LuxeFashion provides international shipping to most countries worldwide. However, please note that shipping fees and delivery times may vary depending on the destination. We strive to ensure a smooth delivery process for all our customers globally.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods to accommodate our customers' preferences. These include major credit cards such as Visa, Mastercard, American Express, as well as popular online payment platforms like PayPal and Apple Pay. Additionally, we offer secure checkout options for a seamless transaction experience.

How can I track my order?

Tracking your order with LuxeFashion is simple and convenient. Once your order has been processed and shipped, you will receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status and whereabouts of your package through our website's tracking portal or directly through the shipping carrier's website.

What is your return policy?

At LuxeFashion, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Please ensure that the item is in its original condition with all tags attached. For detailed instructions on how to initiate a return, please visit our Returns & Exchanges page.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely. LuxeFashion is committed to offering only authentic and genuine luxury clothing and branded items. We source our products directly from authorized distributors and reputable suppliers to guarantee their authenticity. Each item undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our high standards before being made available for purchase on our website.
